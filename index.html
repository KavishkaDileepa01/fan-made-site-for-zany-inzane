About Zany Inzane

Not just a channel name. A Sri Lankan rap voice with videos built for replay.

Zany Inzane is a Sri Lankan hip-hop artist with a verified official YouTube channel carrying dozens of uploads across official music videos, audio releases, lyric videos, and freestyle cuts. His catalog moves through Sinhala rap, melodic hooks, street-minded writing, and sharp visual releases.

Standout videos from the channel and wider catalog include Numbe Ras with Dilo, Rawatavi As, Thiyariya with Leo Leopard and Pridy Boy, Madira with KhShushii, Pubudu, and Dhoomakethu. The sound is local, direct, and built around memorable Sinhala rap delivery with a modern island edge.
